This Document

This document is the complete content guide for the Corner Stone website. It goes section by section — every word, every field, every piece of copy is written here exactly as it should appear on the website.

The reference website to draw inspiration from is bravebrand.pl/en/brave-brand — you've already seen it. This document won't repeat those design decisions. It focuses purely on content, structure, and what goes where.

02 — Navigation Menu
Menu
Structure

The nav bar sits fixed at the top of every page. It has three elements: the logo on the left, and two things on the right — a "Book a Call" button and the menu trigger.
